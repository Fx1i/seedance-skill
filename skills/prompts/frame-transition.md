# 帧到帧平滑过渡提示词模板

## 模板 1：人物微动表现
```
A smooth, natural video transition between the first and last frame showing young girl kids. The girls gently move, blink, and smile with soft, realistic facial expressions. Subtle head and hand movements add life, with natural body motion and calm energy.
```

### 中文描述
在显示小女孩的第一帧和最后一帧之间进行平滑、自然的视频过渡。女孩们轻柔地移动、眨眼和微笑，带有柔和、真实的面部表情。微妙的头部和手部动作增添活力，具有自然的身体动作和平静的能量。

### 优化建议
- 将"young girl kids"替换为你的主体（"a robot"，"a chef"，"a mascot"）
- 保持动作描述温和：眨眼、呼吸、轻微转头这类细节
- 如果过渡不够平滑，添加"seamless interpolation between frames"
- 如果动作幅度太小，明确"slightly more pronounced natural movements"

---

## 模板 2：产品展示过渡
```
A smooth product reveal transitioning between initial and final presentation frames. The product rotates gently with subtle lighting changes, highlighting different features. Clean, professional presentation with smooth camera movement and consistent brand positioning.
```

### 中文描述
在初始和最终展示帧之间进行平滑的产品展示过渡。产品轻柔旋转，伴有微妙的光线变化，突出不同特点。干净、专业的展示，带有流畅的相机移动和一致的品牌定位。

### 优化建议
- 如果产品展示不够清晰，添加"product details remain sharp and in focus"
- 如果品牌定位不一致，使用参考素材@BrandPositioning（品牌定位）
- 如果光线变化不够自然，改为"subtle, realistic lighting transitions"

---

## 模板 3：场景自然演化
```
A natural scene evolution from daytime to sunset between two frames. The lighting gradually shifts from bright daylight to warm golden hour. Trees and foliage subtly sway in the breeze, clouds move slowly across the sky, creating a peaceful temporal transition.
```

### 中文描述
在两帧之间从白天到日落的自然场景演化。光线从明亮的日光逐渐转变为温暖的黄金时刻。树木和树叶在微风中轻柔摇曳，云朵缓慢飘过天空，创造出宁静的时间过渡。

### 优化建议
- 如果时间过渡不够明显，添加"noticeable but natural color temperature shift"
- 如果元素运动不够自然，明确"realistic movement patterns for wind and clouds"
- 使用参考素材：@DayScene（白天场景）、@SunsetScene（日落场景）

---

## 模板 4：表情变化过渡
```
A subtle facial expression transition from neutral to happy expression. The eyes gradually crinkle at the corners, mouth forms into a natural smile, head tilts slightly. Smooth muscle movements maintaining realistic facial anatomy and authentic emotional progression.
```

### 中文描述
从中性到愉快表情的微妙面部表情过渡。眼睛逐渐在眼角起皱，嘴巴形成自然的微笑，头部轻微倾斜。平滑的肌肉运动保持真实面部解剖结构和真实的情感进展。

### 优化建议
- 如果表情变化不自然，添加"gradual, authentic emotional progression"
- 如果面部解剖结构不保持，明确"consistent facial structure throughout transition"
- 使用参考素材：@NeutralFace（中性表情）、@HappyFace（愉快表情）

---

## 模板 5：物体变形过渡
```
A smooth object transformation between two states with natural morphing. The surface flows and reshapes seamlessly, maintaining texture quality and material properties. Fluid motion with believable physics and consistent volume preservation.
```

### 中文描述
在两个状态之间进行平滑的物体变形，带有自然变形。表面流动和重塑无缝衔接，保持纹理质量和材质属性。带有可信物理和一致体积保持的流畅运动。

### 优化建议
- 如果变形不够自然，添加"organic morphing with smooth interpolation"
- 如果材质质量下降，明确"texture and material quality preserved throughout"
- 使用参考素材：@InitialState（初始状态）、@FinalState（最终状态）
